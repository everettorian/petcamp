Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my saint bernard who weighs 180lbs?
There are no breed, size, or weight restrictions at PetCamp! The only pets that are not allowed are venomous and or/illegal pets.
How do I sign up for boarding?
Head on over to the Boarding tab to schedule a reservation.
Is the laundromat free?
Yes, all of the shared amenities are included in your stay.
How tall is the big cat tree?
Over 30 feet tall! We're very proud of the Guiness World Record we received in 2021.
